## Common topics

### Sign-in and account

- Use the same email you registered with. If you use **Sign in with Apple** or **Google**, use that method consistently.
- **Forgot password:** use your provider’s password reset (we don’t store passwords in plain text in the app description).
- **Delete account:** [Describe your process, e.g. “Settings → …” or “Email us from your registered address with ‘Delete my account’.”]

### Subscriptions and billing

- Subscriptions are billed by **Apple** (App Store) or **Google** (Play). Manage or cancel in your device’s subscription settings.
- **Restore purchases:** Use “Restore purchases” on the Plans / subscription screen in the App, if available.
- For **refund** requests, contact Apple or Google support according to their policies.

### Sync and data

- The App can work **offline**; changes may show as **pending sync** until you are online.
- If data doesn’t appear on another device, ensure you’re signed into the **same account** and allow time for sync.

### Team invites

- Invites are sent by email; check spam/junk folders.
- If a link doesn’t work, forward the exact error and the approximate time you tried.

### PDF export and invoices

- PDF export and some features may require an **active subscription**; check your plan in the App.
- Company logo for PDFs is set under **Settings** (owner account).
