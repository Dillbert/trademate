## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Account and authentication

- **Email address** and **display name** when you create an account or sign in (including via third-party sign-in where supported).
- **Authentication identifiers** managed by our identity provider (e.g. Firebase Authentication).

### 2.2 Data you enter in the App

The App is designed for trade and compliance record-keeping. You may store:

- **Client and job information** (e.g. names, addresses, job details, dates, notes).
- **Checklist and inspection data** (e.g. pass/fail, comments).
- **Photos and documents** attached to jobs (e.g. evidence photos, receipts).
- **Signatures** where you use sign-off features.
- **Team and invite information** if you use team features (e.g. invited email addresses, roles).
- **Reminders** linked to clients (e.g. titles, due dates, recurrence).
- **Company profile** (e.g. business name, contact details, company logo image you upload).
- **Invoice data** if you use invoicing (e.g. line items, amounts, notes).

### 2.3 Device and technical data

- **Device type and operating system** (as needed for the App to function).
- **Diagnostic and crash-related data** may be collected by our analytics or crash-reporting tools if enabled in your build.
 
### 2.4 Purchases

If you subscribe through the App Store or Google Play:

- **Purchase status** and **subscription entitlements** are processed by Apple/Google and, where we use a subscription service (e.g. RevenueCat), by that provider according to their terms and privacy policy.
